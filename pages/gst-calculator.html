Understanding GST

GST (Goods and Services Tax) is an indirect tax implemented in India on July 1, 2017. It replaced multiple taxes like VAT, Service Tax, and Excise Duty, creating a unified tax system across the country.

When calculating GST, you need to understand two scenarios:

GST Exclusive

When the price doesn't include GST. The tax is added on top of the base price. Total = Base Price + GST Amount

GST Inclusive

When the price already includes GST. The base price is calculated by removing the tax component. Base = Total / (1 + GST%)

CGST + SGST

For intra-state transactions, GST is split equally between Central GST (CGST) and State GST (SGST).

IGST

For inter-state transactions, Integrated GST (IGST) is charged, which equals CGST + SGST combined.
