⚠️ Late Filing Penalties

Late filing attracts penalty of ₹50/day (₹25 CGST + ₹25 SGST) for GSTR-3B and ₹200/day for GSTR-1 (max ₹10,000). Nil returns have ₹20/day penalty. Additionally, 18% interest is charged on unpaid tax.

Our GST Filing Process

  • Data Collection: Collect sales/purchase invoices, credit/debit notes
  • Data Verification: Match invoices with GSTR-2A/2B for ITC
  • Return Preparation: Prepare GSTR-1 and GSTR-3B accurately
  • Review: Final review before submission
  • Filing: Submit return on GST portal
  • Payment: Generate challan for tax payment
  • Confirmation: Share ARN and filing confirmation

Documents Required

  • Sales invoices (B2B and B2C)
  • Purchase invoices (for ITC)
  • Credit notes and debit notes
  • E-way bills generated
  • Bank statement for payment reconciliation
  • HSN/SAC wise summary

Common GST Filing Mistakes We Help Avoid

  • Mismatch between GSTR-1 and GSTR-3B
  • Incorrect HSN codes
  • Wrong place of supply
  • Claiming ineligible ITC
  • Missing reverse charge transactions
  • Incorrect tax rate application
  • Late filing and penalties

QRMP Scheme Benefits

Businesses with turnover up to ₹5 crore can opt for the Quarterly Return Monthly Payment (QRMP) scheme:

  • File GSTR-1 and GSTR-3B quarterly
  • Pay tax monthly using PMT-06
  • Reduced compliance burden
  • Invoice Furnishing Facility (IFF) for B2B supplies
