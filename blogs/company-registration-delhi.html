Types of Company Registration

Private Limited Company

Starting ₹7,999

Best for startups and businesses seeking investment

LLP Registration

Starting ₹5,999

Ideal for professional services and partnerships

Documents Required for Company Registration

For Directors/Partners:

  • PAN Card (mandatory)
  • Aadhaar Card
  • Passport size photographs
  • Address proof (Voter ID/Passport/Driving License)
  • Bank statement or utility bill (not older than 2 months)

For Registered Office:

  • Electricity/Water bill (not older than 2 months)
  • Property ownership document OR
  • Rent agreement with NOC from landlord

Post-Registration Compliances

After company registration, certain compliances are mandatory:

  • PAN & TAN: Obtained along with incorporation
  • Bank Account: Open current account in company name
  • GST Registration: Required if turnover exceeds threshold
  • Professional Tax: State-specific registration
  • MSME/Udyam: Recommended for benefits
  • Shops & Establishment: Local labor law registration
