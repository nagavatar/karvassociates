Documents Required for ITR Filing

For Salaried Individuals:

  • PAN Card
  • Aadhaar Card
  • Form 16 from employer
  • Bank statements (all accounts)
  • Investment proofs (80C, 80D, etc.)
  • Interest certificates (FD, savings)
  • Home loan certificate (if applicable)
  • Form 26AS (Tax Credit Statement)

For Business Owners:

  • All above documents
  • Books of accounts
  • Sales and purchase invoices
  • GST returns
  • Bank statements (current account)
  • Depreciation schedule

Tax Saving Tips for Noida Residents

  • Section 80C: Invest up to ₹1.5L in PPF, ELSS, NSC, life insurance
  • Section 80D: Health insurance premium deduction up to ₹75,000
  • Section 24: Home loan interest deduction up to ₹2L
  • Section 80E: Education loan interest - no upper limit
  • NPS (80CCD): Additional ₹50,000 deduction under 80CCD(1B)
  • HRA: Claim HRA exemption if paying rent
