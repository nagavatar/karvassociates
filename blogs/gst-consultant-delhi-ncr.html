Frequently Asked Questions

What is the GST registration threshold?

For goods: ₹40 lakhs annual turnover. For services: ₹20 lakhs annual turnover. Some states have lower thresholds of ₹20 lakhs and ₹10 lakhs respectively.

What are the due dates for GST returns?

GSTR-1: 11th of the following month. GSTR-3B: 20th of the following month. GSTR-9: 31st December of the next financial year.

Can you help with GST notices?

Yes, we provide complete support for responding to GST notices, including scrutiny notices, demand notices, and show cause notices.
