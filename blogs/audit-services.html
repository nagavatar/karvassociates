Assurance & Compliance

Audit & Assurance

Independent, thorough audits conducted with precision, integrity, and complete confidentiality.

A well-conducted audit builds stakeholder confidence and ensures regulatory compliance. Our experienced audit team brings objectivity, expertise, and attention to detail to every engagement.

Statutory Audit

Mandatory annual audits under the Companies Act, 2013. We verify financial statements and compliance with accounting standards.

Tax Audit (Sec 44AB)

Required for businesses with turnover exceeding prescribed limits. Form 3CA/3CB and Form 3CD preparation.

Internal Audit

Evaluate internal controls, risk management, and operational efficiency with actionable recommendations.

GST Audit

Comprehensive review of GST compliance, ITC claims, and reconciliations to ensure accuracy.

Bank Audit

Specialized audits of banking institutions including branch audits and concurrent audits per RBI guidelines.

Due Diligence

Financial and legal due diligence for mergers, acquisitions, and investments.
