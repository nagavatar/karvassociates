Old vs New Tax Regime - Which is Better?

Old Tax Regime

Traditional regime with deductions

  • 80C, 80D, HRA deductions available
  • Home loan interest deduction
  • Standard deduction ₹50,000
  • Best if you have investments
  • Higher rates: 5%-30%

New Tax Regime (Default)

Simplified with lower rates

  • No major deductions
  • Standard deduction ₹75,000
  • Lower tax slabs
  • Best if minimal investments
  • Rates: 5%-30% (lower brackets)

Tax Planning Strategies for Individuals

  • Maximize 80C: Invest the full ₹1.5 lakh in PPF, ELSS, NPS, or insurance
  • Health Insurance: Buy medical insurance for family and parents (₹75,000 deduction)
  • Home Loan: Claim interest up to ₹2 lakh and principal under 80C
  • NPS Investment: Additional ₹50,000 under 80CCD(1B)
  • HRA Planning: Restructure salary to maximize HRA exemption
  • Capital Gains: Time your investments to utilize LTCG exemption

Tax Planning for Businesses

  • Entity Structuring: Choose the right business structure (proprietorship, partnership, LLP, company)
  • Depreciation Planning: Maximize depreciation claims on assets
  • Expense Timing: Time major expenses for tax efficiency
  • Presumptive Taxation: Opt for 44AD/44ADA if beneficial
  • GST Input Credit: Ensure proper ITC utilization
  • Advance Tax: Avoid interest by timely quarterly payments
